Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

A list of General Questions we've been asked

  • Q1: What is MoneyWise Kids?
    A: MoneyWise Kids is an educational platform that helps children and teens learn about money management through games, challenges, and real-world simulations.
  • Q2: How can I sign my child up?
    A: You can sign up by visiting our Learning Portals page and selecting the appropriate age group for your child.
  • Q3: Is MoneyWise Kids free to use?
    A: Yes! Our core content is free to access, though some premium features may be available in the future.
  • Q4: Can educators use MoneyWise Kids in the classroom?
    A: Absolutely. Teachers can use our Educator Dashboard to monitor student progress and access classroom resources.
  • Q5: How can I volunteer or become a sponsor?
    A: Use the form below to express your interest in volunteering or sponsorship opportunities, and our team will get in touch.
  • Q6: Is the content age-appropriate?
    A: Yes, we’ve carefully designed separate learning portals for ages 6–10, 11–14, and 15–17 to ensure age-appropriate material.
