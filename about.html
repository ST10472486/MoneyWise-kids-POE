Our History

MoneyWise Kids was launched in 2025 by a team of educators, financial experts, and game designers to address the lack of early financial education. The platform makes learning about money exciting through games, challenges, and real-world simulations.

Mission

To empower the next generation with financial confidence by teaching money skills early through play, practice, and real-life application.

Vision

A future where every child grows up financially literate, capable of making smart money decisions and building wealth from a young age.
